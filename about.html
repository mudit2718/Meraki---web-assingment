📖 Our Story

Established in 2020, Meraki is a dream carved in flavors and aromas. We envisioned a space where creativity, community, and cuisine blend into unforgettable experiences.

🌍 Our Vibe

Nestled opposite ICAI Bhawan, our café is a warm, cozy world of rustic interiors, artisanal lighting, green corners, and an ever-present aroma of fresh brews and baked goodness.

💫 Our Vision

To be Indore’s most loved vegetarian café — where every dish tells a story and every moment creates a memory.

🧡 Our Core Values

  • Creativity & authenticity in food
  • Community-first approach
  • Commitment to excellence & hygiene
  • Respect for local, sustainable ingredients

👨‍🍳 Meet Our Chef

Chef Aarav Singh — The heart behind Meraki’s kitchen magic. With 12+ years of culinary artistry, Chef Aarav blends tradition, innovation, and soul into every plate.
